WHAT IS SKOOLL NFT?

SKOOLL NFT is a new concept that gives you the opportunity to earn money passively with just one token.

Buy and Earn

NFT SKOOLL collection should give you the feeling that you are holding not just a token that represents
nothing, but a real asset.
EARN SKOOLL NFT
Mint NFT on this website. The more NFT you have, the more chances you have to
earn. You can also sell NFTs on the marketplace and earn money.
HOLD SKOOLL NFT
The more NFTs you hold, the more profit it makes. Each Skooll is a pass to participate in future and current events.
GET PASSIVE INCOME
Each month a percentage of that month's sales will be raffled off between NFT owners. In other words, the higher the monthly trading volume of the Skooll nft collection, the more money will be raffled off.
The token can be bought and resold on OpenSea
